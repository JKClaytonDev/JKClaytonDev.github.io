CASTILLO: Shattered Mirrors was a First Person Shooter with Collectathon elements. The game has a structure close to Super Mario Galaxy, it contains a hub world split into 3 sections, each with worlds and missions that can be played in any order. I worked alongside a voice actor and multiple playtesters, after gathering playtesting feedback, I encountered multiple playtesters getting stuck on a single mission and quitting, I then incorporatead a life counter where game over forces a return to the hub to prevent this.

Accomplishments:
  • Shipped on Steam after conducting a social media marketing campaign, writing a press kit, and working with a voice actor.
  • Utilized lighting temperature differences to direct towards secrets, wrote a custom environment shader to make the baked lighting compliment the art style.
  • Crafted environments that support engaging gameplay with scripted dynamic level events and setpieces.
  • Level design accounts possibility of out of order play while also featuring a heavy focus on visual worldbuilding.
