TLDR: Solo project shipped on Steam, 3D platformer

A solo project with outsourced music. It's an FPS Platformer I used to test my level design skills by making a multi-hour game accessible to all players.

Accomplishments:
  • Prioritized level flow with vertically aligned routes; lower paths are easier but slower. Implemented a boost system to discourage skipping arenas.
  • Developed AI navigation for high entity counts on low-end hardware, and crafted a custom level editor.
  • Sketched concept art in Illustrator and modeled in Maya.
  • Enhanced atmosphere with Unity's HDRP.
  • Released on Steam, iterated through playtests for user feedback.
