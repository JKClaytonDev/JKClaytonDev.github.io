Flying Frags World Tour is a FPS Platformer that was largely a solo project, but I hired a music composer and a humanoid character modeler for the player in cutscenes. Role: Level Designer, Programmer, Game Designer, Lighting Designer, Environment Artist… (everything but music and one character model) Stuff I did: Shipped on Steam and conducted playtests while properly incorporating feedback. Produced environment concept art before modeling props in Maya. Utilizing dynamic shadows and volumetric fog in Unity’s High Definition Render Pipeline to enhance atmosphere. Focused on level flow, utilizing multiple vertically aligned routes, lowest being easiest but slowest. Incorporated a speed boost combo system that incentivises players to not run past combat sequences. Created enemy design philosophy that pairs movement type, difficulty, and attack type with animation, size, and shape accordingly. Programmed AI navigation system to account for high enemy counts on low-end hardware and created a custom level editor that parses levels into files.
