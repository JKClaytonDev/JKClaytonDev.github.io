Flying Frags World Tour is a FPS Platformer that was largely a solo project, but I hired a music composer and a humanoid character modeler for the player in cutscenes. I wanted to test my level design skills and make a multi-hour game accessible to all types of players.

Accomplishments:
  • Shipped on Steam and conducted playtests while properly incorporating feedback.
  • Produced environment concept art before modeling props in Maya. Utilizing dynamic shadows and volumetric fog in Unityâ€™s High Definition Render Pipeline to enhance atmosphere.
  • Focused on level flow, utilizing multiple vertically aligned routes, lowest being easiest but slowest. Incorporated a speed boost combo system to prevent skipping of that incentivises players to not skip combat sequences.
  • Created enemy design philosophy that pairs movement type, difficulty, and attack type with animation, size, and shape.
  • Programmed AI navigation system to account for high enemy counts on low-end hardware and created a custom level editor that parses levels into files.
