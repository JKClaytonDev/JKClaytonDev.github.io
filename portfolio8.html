Game Design Document + Demo - Mecha Crawl

This was a game I made for the Miami University Game Design Club. It is a VR Game where you play as a giant fish escaping other Kaiju. I worked as the designer/programmer.



GAME CONCEPT

VR game combining exploration, puzzles, and action, where players control a fish-operated robot, changing perspectives by lifting their fishbowl to navigate and using a laser cube for defense.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Players who enjoy innovative gameplay mechanics, combining exploration, strategy, and action.


GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

Control a robot from a fish's perspective inside a bowl. Navigate by lifting the bowl for movement, drop it to grab a laser cube for combat against enemies, and solve puzzles by altering perspectives. Balance moving and fighting for strategic gameplay.




GAME WORLD

A linear, kaiju-infested city.

ENEMIES

Kaiju enemies with diverse strengths and tactics challenge players to strategically defeat them using the laser cube.



OBJECTIVES

Navigate, solve puzzles, and combat kaiju.
