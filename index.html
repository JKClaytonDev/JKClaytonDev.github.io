I grew up in northern Virginia and have been teaching myself how to make video games since I got a 9-inch Intel Atom Disney netbook for my tenth birthday. I am currently studying Games and Simulations at Miami University and am president of the Game Design Club where we collaborate to make a game from start to finish in under 4 hours.

I have shipped PC and Mobile games using Unity and Unreal 5 and am teaching myself Unreal C++. I've released five games on Steam, two featured in Reload Magazine. I like to mix genres in my indie games. I am also proficient in level, lighting, and environment design, with experience creating scenes in software like Maya and Blender or whiteboxing in-engine using ProBuilder and Cube Grid.

During my free time, I've been kickflipping my way through Tony Hawks Pro Skater 1+2, fragging in Quake Live, and missing my shots as Raze in Valorant. I also love working out, jogging, and the outdoors, whether hiking or camping.
