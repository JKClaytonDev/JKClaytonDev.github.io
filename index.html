My name is Jackson Clayton. I am a Games major at Miami University. I grew up in northern Virginia, I've been teaching myself game design and development since I got a Disney netbook on my tenth birthday.

I am a passionate game designer and developer who focuses on mixing genres. I've released 5 games on Steam and was featured in Reload Magazine. I've shipped PC and Mobile games using Unity and Unreal 5 and am currently teaching myself Unreal C++.

I am also proficient in level, lighting and environment design, and have experience both creating scenes in Maya and Blender and also whiteboxing in-engine using ProBuilder and Cube Grid.

When I'm not making games, I love to go out with friends, record videos, and go running.
