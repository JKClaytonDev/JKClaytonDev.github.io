Game Design:

CASTILLO: The Nine Circles is a collectathon, so I had to change many different gameplay elements while also having the gameplay add onto the first game for players who complete them in order.

Level Design:

The level design required me to totally rethink my process when making open ended levels with multiple different challenges. I had to change how I taught mechanics through level design and made sure to emphasize player freedom.

Character Design:

I had to spend lots of time creating characters that fit the general tone of the game and didn't seem out of place while also making them distinct from those of the first game.
