Mega Battle Royale - (In Progress)

Objective 1: Study Flow/Engagement Design in Battle Royales

Objective 2: Learn about optimizing memory usage in UEFN




POI 1

Rich in loot but risky due to sparse cover, this area offers a deceptive mix of tranquility and danger. I positioned structures to support defensive plays, yet the gaps between them require quick movement to mitigate exposure to snipers and surprise attacks.

POI 2

This dense city setting with vertical gameplay emphasizing rooftop exploration and control has varied building heights to encourage high ground advantage for recon and sniping, while tight alleys and interiors foster intense close-quarters combat. Accessible rooftops facilitate strategic ambushes and escapes.


POI 3

This zone features a power plant designed for intense close-quarters battles, fostering dynamic clashes amid industrial gear and tight passages. Its compact layout of generators and panels encourages quick moves, while limited visibility tests long-distance strategies. The blend of indoor and outdoor settings requires fast adjustments to diverse fight situations, from outdoor vulnerabilities to indoor high-stakes zones.




POI 4

This spot is a low-risk start with limited loot, perfect for strategic play. Its spread-out buildings and walkways make for quick looting and fewer early fights, offering clear paths and lower ambush chances. The area's lack of top-tier gear pushes players to use the terrain and buildings for silent, efficient gathering before moving on. It rewards stealth and survival skills, setting up a game that slowly ramps up in intensity.

POI 5

Located on a cliff's edge, this area balances high-reward loot against the risk of limited escape paths. The adjacent cable bridge serves as either a strategic exit or a potential trap, depending on combat situations. The layout promotes intense engagements, utilizing both interior and exterior settings for tactical maneuvers. Designed for players adept in combat and swift decision-making.



POI 6

This environment showcases a broad landing area, fostering intense skirmishes that merge traditional and vertical combat amidst varied architectural designs. It features open courtyards and layered buildings, catering to both outdoor and indoor engagements. The abundance of loot necessitates cautious movement in exposed areas. Diverse building heights and sightlines create unique combat situations, compelling players to adapt to evolving enemy tactics.


POI 7

This section highlights vertical gameplay to foster rapid, close-quarter engagements. Multi-level designs provide ample ambush points and tactical locations, with constrained ground space pushing exploration upward for strategic advantage. Compact areas increase encounter rates and intensity. Accessible rooftops and linked pathways promote swift, seamless movement. Players are compelled to maintain constant vigilance and adeptly maneuver through vertical spaces to outwit rivals.


Full Map

The design strategically distributes loot across key areas, leveraging verticality for positional advantages and choke points for effective storm evasion. It enhances engagement and strategic depth by placing premium loot at varied elevations. The terrain encourages dynamic combat and environmental tactics. Navigational challenges at choke points enhance storm avoidance strategies.
