I worked alongside the HYPERJUSTICE team to complete this “anti-bullet time” third person shooter autorunner during the 90 Day To Ship It Jam. The team was very proud of the final product so we polished it a bit more after and put it on Steam. I worked as a programmer and game, level, and lighting designer. Role: Level Designer, Game Designer, Programmer, Lighting Designer. Responsibilities: Shipped on Steam with a small team utilizing Trello for task management. Level design rewards speed and reaction time while emphasizing the level specific weapon’s attributes. Bounceable grenades reward creative thinking. Lighting temperature and range signify local gameplay scenarios, with light drafts acting as foreshadowing. Worked alongside a narrative designer and concept artist when decorating environments. Incorporated a style boost mechanic for dodging bullets, rewarding speed and variety.
